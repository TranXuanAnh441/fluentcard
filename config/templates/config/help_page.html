Welcome to FluentCard, a ChatGPT-powered Flashcard Website !

Unlike other flashcard applications, we use Generative AI to enhance vocabulary learning experience

  • ChatGPT-generated exercise: let's review the flashcards by AI-generated exercise, only memorize but understand vocabulary better.
  • Dictionary: let's search for words in our built-in dictionary with official data from Jisho.org, create flashcards from words directly without extra typing.
  • RolePlay: let's practice using the words you are learning in communication, you can choose or create your own roleplay prompts.
